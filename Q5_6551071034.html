EAT HEALTHY LIVE HEALTHY

To eat and live healthily, you should prioritize fruits, vegetables, whole grains, plant proteins, fish and low-fat dairy products, while limiting processed foods, foods high in sugar, salt, saturated fats and trans fats. Maintaining a balanced diet, drinking enough water and combining it with regular exercise are effective ways to achieve a healthy weight, improve physical and mental health and prevent disease.
